# Values & Principles

## Core Values
- Building in public — share the journey, not just the outcome
- Community over competition
- Thoughtful engineering — measure twice, cut once
- Hackathons as systems, not just events
- Faith and gratitude

## Decision-Making
- Favor action over analysis paralysis, but not at the cost of quality
- Prefer explicit over clever
- Handle more edge cases rather than fewer
- Ask "what would I want if I were the user?"

## What Matters
- Immigration stability (O-1 visa)
- Building products that serve communities
- Growing the DayDreamers and MentorMates ecosystems
- Personal growth through reflection (Obsidian, daily digest)
